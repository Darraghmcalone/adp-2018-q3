// Scenarios using BDD

Title:

A data consultant understand more about their students.

Narrative:

As a data consultant at a coding school called Rouge Academie 
I want to know statistical data 
so that i can understand more about my students.

Scenario:

Given [students have more than 3 years of experience]
When [students have a score over 4 of satisfaction]
And [students have 100% attendance]
Then [do they pass every project]


Given [students have less than 1 years of experience]
When [students have a score less than 2 of satisfaction]
And [students have less than 80% attendance]
Then [do they fail the first project]
